# Pig Dice Game

> A two-player browser implementation of the classic Pig dice game, built with vanilla JavaScript.

## How to Play

Pig is a simple two-player dice game where the first player to reach 100 points wins.

On your turn you can:

- **Roll** the dice. If you roll anything other than 1, the number is added to your round score. If you roll a 1, you lose your round score and it becomes the other player's turn.
- **Hold** to bank your round score into your total. The turn then passes to the other player.

The first player to reach 100 total points wins.

## Getting Started

No build step required. Just open `index.html` in your browser.

```bash
# Or serve it locally
npx serve .
```
